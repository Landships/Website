What is Project:Landships?

Project:Landships is an undergraduate research project by UC Berkeley's VR@Berkeley club set on creating a compelling VR multiplayer experience that puts players and their friends in the roles of a tank, challenging them to work together and fight opposing tanks.

Tank Simulation

Experience operating a WW2 Sherman Firefly from inside the cockpit!

Multiplayer Crew

You and your friends fill the historical roles of driver, gunner, loader, and spotter.

Virtual Reality

We deliver unprecedented immersion using new VR technology like the Oculus Rift or the HTC Vive.

Progress so far

  • End of January 2016

    Pre-semester stage - Complete

    - We got the Oculus Rift and Playstation Move Controllers working with Unity.
    - Basic networking functions for multiplayer were figured out.
    - We decided on game mechanics and setting.
    - Basic tank functions and controls using VR were completed.
    - We also created basic tank exterior and interior designs.

  • End of Feburary 2016

    Onboarding Demo

    - Simple working demo showing Move controllers working in conjunction with the Oculus Rift.
    - Players could drive the tank using in-game directional blocks. The turret could be controlled using buttons on the Playstation Move ctonroller.
    - For the demo environment we created a desert scene populated with alien broccoli monsters that players could attack.

  • End of March 2016

    Mid-Semester Demo

    - Implemented Chobi Atelier's Physics Tank Simulator asset package, which helps us with exterior models, tank physics, track physics, and damage calculation.
    - Now switched to testing on the Vive Pre instead of the Oculus Rift.
    - Using the Sherman Firefly model, we now have working interior model, VR lever drive controls, VR hand models, tank damage UI, hatch implementation, AI tanks, and periscope.

  • End of April 2016

    End-of-Semester Demo

    - We hope to have completed multiplayer working with the Vive.
    - We are aiming to have fully functional driver and shooter positions.
    - Players will be able to form 2-person teams and engage with other 2-person teams in the game.
